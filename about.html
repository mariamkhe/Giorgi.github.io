Architect

Giorgi Gvaramadze

Giorgi Gvaramadze is an experienced architect, artist, and designer. He graduated from Georgian technical university with a major in architecture, urban planning, and design. He is famous for his excellent eye for detail.
Giorgi Gvaramadze was the first student that had the chance to take part in the restoration of Georgian's most famous building: Ivane Javakhishvili Tbilisi State University. also, he was taken part of renovation the movie theatre Apolo.
At this point, Giorgi Gvaramadze owns one of the successful architecture companies 'DBL'. This company develops day by day and stands on the road to big success.
